The Student Reception

The Student Reception located on the ground floor of University House acts as a ‘one stop shop’ for students wishing to access the University’s support services. The Student Reception is able to offer advice and guidance in the following areas:

Student Development and Support Reception

The Student Development and Support (SDS) Reception is the first point of contact for visitors to any of the following services:

Careers Service

The Senior Tutor and Counseling Service

The Disability Co-ordinator

The Warwick Skills Certificate and Graduate Skills Programme

Personal Development Planning Co-ordinator

Student Funding

Head of Residential Life 

The SDS reception, working closely with the Careers Service, is also able to assist students with:

·         Booking appointments with Careers and Application Advisors

·         Mock Interviews

·         Aptitude Tests

·         Workshops

·         Employer Presentations

·         Finding online careers information

·         Locating the Careers Hub

SDS Reception can be contacted on 02476 575508 or by e-mail: studentdevelopmentandsupport@warwick.ac.uk

Academic Office Reception

The Academic Office Reception acts as first point of contact for the following services:

Student Finance

Student Records   

The Graduate School

Student Recruitment and Admissions

Events and Enquires

Examinations

Enrolment

Degree Congregation

The reception offers advice and guidance to students for all the above departments including the processing of fee payments and the issuing of student status letters.   

Unitemps 

Unitemps is an online temporary staff agency established by the University of Warwick.For details of Unitemps and how to register please see the following link: https://www.unitemps.co.uk/public/default.asp

The Reception Team

The Student Reception offers a friendly and professional service. The Reception Team is equipped to offer advice and guidance relating to all areas of the Academic Office and Student Development and Support Services. We welcome feedback from students on our operations and this can be given via the feedback forms in the reception or via link. 

Opening Hours

The Student Reception is open from 9.00am-5.00pm Monday to Thursday and from 9.00am-4.00pm on Fridays. Please note that payments will not be processed after 4.30pm Monday-Thursday and 3.30pm on Fridays.
